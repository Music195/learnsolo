Sequence Sum and General Term

Core Concept: Partial Sums and General Terms

Partial sums provide a powerful connection between sequences and their general terms. By understanding how partial sums relate to individual terms, we can find general terms and solve complex sequence problems.

Partial Sum Definition

$S_n = \sum_{k=1}^n a_k = a_1 + a_2 + \dots + a_n$

Where:
$S_n$ = sum of first n terms
$a_k$ = kth term of the sequence

General Term from Partial Sums

$a_n = S_n - S_{n-1}$

Each term is the difference between consecutive partial sums

Key Relationships

Term-Sum Relationships

The relationship between terms and partial sums:

$a_1 = S_1$
$a_n = S_n - S_{n-1} \quad (n \geq 2)$

This allows us to find individual terms from partial sums

Sum-Term Relationships

The relationship between partial sums and terms:

$S_n = S_{n-1} + a_n \quad (n \geq 2)$
$S_n = \sum_{k=1}^n a_k$

This allows us to find partial sums from individual terms

Connection to Series

Mathematical Context: Partial sums are fundamental to the study of infinite series. The behavior of partial sums as n approaches infinity determines whether a series converges or diverges. Understanding finite partial sums builds the foundation for infinite series analysis.
