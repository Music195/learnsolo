Exponent Rules
a, b are positive integers.
  1. $x^a \times x^b = $ $x^{a+b} $
  2. $(x^a)^b = $ $x^{ab} $
  3. $(xy)^b = $ $x^b y^b $
💡
For example, as $x^3$ and $x^2$,
$x^3 \times x^2 = (x \times x \times x) \times (x \times x) = x^{3+2} = x^5$
$(x^3)^2 = (x \times x \times x) \times (x \times x \times x) = x^{3 \times 2} = x^6$
$(xy)^2 = (xy) \times (xy) = (x \times x) \times (y \times y) = x^2 y^2$
