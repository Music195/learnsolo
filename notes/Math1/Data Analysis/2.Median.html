Median (中央値, メジアン)

Data Analysis
When data is arranged in order of size, the value in the middle position is called the median or メジアン.

If the number of data points is even, the median is the average of the two central values.
Odd Number of Data
↑
Median
Even Number of Data
↑
Average of the two central values is the median
💡 The median is less affected by extreme values (outliers) in the data, making it a suitable representative value in such cases.
